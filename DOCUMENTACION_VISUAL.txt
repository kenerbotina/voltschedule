## 🎭 ANIMACIONES (Framer Motion)

### Tipos de Animaciones
- **Fade In**: `initial={{ opacity: 0 }} animate={{ opacity: 1 }}`
- **Slide In**: `initial={{ opacity: 0, y: 24 }} animate={{ opacity: 1, y: 0 }}`
- **Scale**: `initial={{ opacity: 0, scale: 1.04 }} animate={{ opacity: 0.75, scale: 1 }}`
- **Stagger**: Animaciones secuenciales con `delay`

### Duraciones
- **Rápida**: `duration: 0.2` (200ms)
- **Normal**: `duration: 0.3` (300ms)
- **Lenta**: `duration: 0.6` (600ms)
- **Muy Lenta**: `duration: 1.1` (1100ms)

### Easing
- **Suave**: `ease: [0.16, 1, 0.3, 1]` (cubic-bezier)
- **Lineal**: `ease: "linear"`
- **Ease Out**: `ease: "easeOut"`

### Transiciones CSS
- **Hover**: `transition-all duration-200`
- **Transform**: `transition-transform duration-300`
- **Colors**: `transition-colors duration-200`
